Can I evaluate Adhibit without Pro?

Yes. Library, history panel, Pinboards, privacy controls, settings, and unlock options remain visible. Pro is required when you use capture, paste, organize, import, or sync actions.

Why does direct paste ask for permission?

macOS requires Accessibility access before an app can insert content into another app. Without it, Adhibit copies clips back to the system clipboard first.

Does Adhibit need an account?

No. The app is designed to work local-first. Optional iCloud sync and Paste import are user-initiated features.
